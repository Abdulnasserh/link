# 🎥 Link: Demo Video Script (Finale Demo Version)

## [0:00-0:15] The Hook: The Black Hole
**Visual**: Dark screen with hospital monitor "beep." Text: "THE POST-DISCHARGE BLACK HOLE."
**Narrator**: "You’ve just been discharged. You're home. But there's a ticking time bomb: 100% of patients leave with pending lab results that no one is watching. These are 'Ghost Results'—critical data that arrives after you've gone home."

## [0:15-0:45] The Problem: Why It Matters
**Visual**: Fast graphics showing: 40% critical results missed. 60% medication errors.
**Narrator**: "In 2026, healthcare is faster than ever, yet patients are falling through the cracks. Medication collisions and missed labs lead to avoidable readmissions and hospital penalties. We built Link to bridge this dangerous gap."

## [0:45-1:20] The Solution: Link & Interoperability
**Visual**: Link Logo (🔗). Architecture overview showing the MCP Server.
**Narrator**: "Link is an interoperable AI safety net. It uses the Model Context Protocol to deploy a 'Digital Sentry' that stays behind at the hospital, continuously monitoring FHIR records for finalized results. No data is stored; Link acts as a real-time, secure bridge between the hospital and the provider."

## [1:20-3:15+] 🚀 GRAND FINALE: Live from Prompt Opinion
**Visual**: **[SWITCH TO PROMPT OPINION PLATFORM]**
*   **Step 1**: Show the Link MCP Server connected in PO.
*   **Step 2**: Enter a prompt: *"Check for any pending lab results for my patient Maria Rivera and prepare the follow-up actions."*
*   **Step 3**: Show Link fetching the results live from the MCP.
*   **Step 4**: **[THE "LOOP-CLOSE"]** Show the Agent's output containing:
    *   The Critical Result analysis.
    *   The drafted **FHIR Task** for the clinic staff.
    *   The **Patient-Friendly Instruction**.
**Narrator (Live or Overlaid)**: *"Now, let's see Link in action on the Prompt Opinion platform. I'm going to ask Link to check for Maria Rivera's pending results. Watch as the MCP server instantly connects to the hospital's FHIR server to fetch the 'Ghost Labs' that were finalized post-discharge. Look at that—it found a critical lab result. But Link doesn't just notify; it acts. Right here in the output, Link has automatically drafted a FHIR Task for the clinic staff to follow up and generated a clear, simple instruction for Maria. This is how we close the loop—turning a 'Ghost Result' into a real-world medical action, all within the Prompt Opinion platform."*

## [Final Scene] Conclusion
**Visual**: Link Logo: "Built for the Prompt Opinion / MCP Hackathon."
**Narrator**: "Link. Bridging the gap. Ensuring no one is ever lost in the black hole again."

### 💡 How to explain "Closing the Loop" in PO:
In the Prompt Opinion UI, you'll show the **text response** from the Link agent. That response should contain:
1.  **Clinical Finding**: "Potassium level critical (6.2 mEq/L)."
2.  **Action Item**: "Drafting FHIR Task: Request immediate follow-up with Maria Rivera."
3.  **Patient Instruction**: "Alert: Your lab results require a medication adjustment. Please call Dr. Smith's office today."

This shows the judges that the "action" is being orchestrated by the AI inside the platform.
